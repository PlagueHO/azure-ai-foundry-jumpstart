PURPOSE:
To provide evidence-based guidance for medication management in Emergency Medicine practice to ensure optimal patient outcomes and standardized care delivery.

SCOPE:
Patient Populations:
- Adult patients presenting with emergency medicine conditions
- Patients requiring medication management intervention

Clinical Settings:
- Emergency Department
- Inpatient Ward
- Outpatient Clinic

Exclusion Criteria:
- Pediatric patients under 18 years
- Patients with multiple comorbidities requiring specialized care

Epidemiology: Approximately 40% of emergency medicine cases require this type of intervention annually.
Current Evidence Summary: Multiple Level II studies demonstrate efficacy of standardized protocols in reducing adverse events and improving patient satisfaction.
